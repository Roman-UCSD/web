Stellar models

47 Tucanae

Evolutionary models and model atmospheres for the members of 47 Tucanae, spanning evolutionary stages from brown dwarfs to the subgiant branch, and the range of oxygen content observed in the cluster. The associated publication is in preparation.

  • Download

Galactic Archaeology with Ultracool Dwarfs

Preliminary version of the grid of evolutionary models and model atmospheres for field brown dwarfs and lower main sequence stars at a variety of chemical compositions. At present, the grid only contains two chemistries that approximately represent thick disk and halo objects. The associated publication is in preparation. More chemistries are on the way!

  • Download

Population III

Model atmospheres for pure hydrogen and helium stars from 1 to 820 solar masses, according to Table 5 of Larkin+2023.

  • Download
  • Publication

ω Centauri

Model atmospheres for the globular cluster ω Centauri. Multiple chemistries are available, according to Tables 1 and 2 of Gerasimov+2022. Brown dwarf models are available for some chemistries, but many have poor convergence.
